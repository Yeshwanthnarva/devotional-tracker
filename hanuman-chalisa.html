Hanuman Chalisa

हनुमान चालीसा — The Complete 40 Verses of Devotion to Lord Hanuman

What is Hanuman Chalisa?
Hanuman Chalisa is a devotional hymn (stotra) dedicated to Lord Hanuman, composed by the great poet-saint Tulsidas in the Awadhi language. The word "Chalisa" means "forty" in Hindi, as the hymn consists of 40 verses (chaupais). It is one of the most popular and powerful prayers in Hinduism, recited daily by millions of devotees for strength, courage, protection, and spiritual upliftment.

🙏 Significance of Hanuman Chalisa

Lord Hanuman, the embodiment of devotion, strength, and selfless service, is worshipped as the destroyer of fears and obstacles. Reciting Hanuman Chalisa is believed to remove fear, anxiety, and negative energies, grant physical and mental strength, protect from evil influences, bestow wisdom, courage, and devotion, help overcome difficult situations, and bring peace, prosperity, and spiritual growth.

✨ Benefits of Reciting Hanuman Chalisa Daily

🛡️ Protection from Evil 💪 Physical & Mental Strength 🧠 Wisdom & Memory 🚫 Removal of Obstacles 🙏 Deep Devotion 🌸 Peace & Prosperity

Regular recitation (1, 3, 7, 11, or 21 times daily) brings numerous benefits: deepens devotion, purifies the mind, removes karmic blocks, reduces stress and anxiety, strengthens the immune system, removes financial obstacles, and provides protection from enemies and dangers.

🌺 Invocation Dohas (Opening Verses)
श्रीगुरु चरन सरोज रज, निज मन मुकुर सुधारि।
बरनउँ रघुबर बिमल जसु, जो दायक फल चारि॥
Shri Guru charan saroj raj, nij man mukuru sudhaari | Barnau Raghubar bimal jasu, jo daayak phal chaari ||
Meaning: After cleansing the mirror of my mind with the dust of my Guru's lotus feet, I sing the pure glory of Lord Rama, the bestower of the four fruits of life (Dharma, Artha, Kama, Moksha).
बुद्धिहीन तनु जानिके, सुमिरौं पवन-कुमार।
बल बुधि बिद्या देहु मोहिं, हरहु कलेस बिकार॥
Buddhiheen tanu jaanike, sumiraun pavan-kumaar | Bal budhi vidya dehu mohi, harahu kales vikaar ||
Meaning: Knowing myself to be devoid of intelligence, I meditate on you, O son of the wind god. Grant me strength, wisdom, and knowledge, and remove my sorrows and impurities.
🙏 Hanuman Chalisa — Complete 40 Chaupais (Main Verses)
जय हनुमान ज्ञान गुन सागर।
जय कपीस तिहुँ लोक उजागर॥1॥
Jai Hanuman gyaan gun saagar | Jai Kapeesh tihu lok ujaagar ||1||
Victory to Hanuman, the ocean of wisdom and virtue! Victory to the lord of monkeys, who illuminates all the three worlds!
राम दूत अतुलित बल धामा।
अंजनि पुत्र पवनसुत नामा॥2॥
Raam doot atulit bal dhaama | Anjani putra Pavan sut naama ||2||
You are the messenger of Lord Rama, the abode of incomparable strength. You are known as the son of Anjani and the son of the wind god.
महाबीर बिक्रम बजरंगी।
कुमति निवार सुमति के संगी॥3॥
Mahaabeer bikram Bajrangi | Kumati nivaar sumati ke sangi ||3||
O great warrior of immense valor, with a body as strong as a thunderbolt! You remove evil intellect and bestow noble wisdom upon your companions.
कंचन बरन बिराज सुबेसा।
कानन कुण्डल कुँचित केसा॥4॥
Kanchan baran biraaj subesha | Kanan kundal kunchit kesa ||4||
You are of golden complexion and adorned with beautiful attire. You wear earrings in your ears and have curly hair.
हाथ बज्र अरु ध्वजा बिराजै।
काँधे मूँज जनेऊ साजै॥5॥
Haath bajra aur dhwaja birajai | Kaandhe moonj janeu sajai ||5||
In your hands shine the thunderbolt (mace) and the flag of victory. You adorn your shoulder with the sacred thread made of munja grass.
शंकर सुवन केसरी नंदन।
तेज प्रताप महा जग बंदन॥6॥
Shankar suvan Kesari nandan | Tej prataap maha jag bandan ||6||
You are the beloved of Shankar (Shiva) and the delight of Kesari. Your radiance and glory are worshipped throughout the world.
बिद्यावान गुनी अति चातुर।
राम काज करिबे को आतुर॥7॥
Vidyaavaan guni ati chaatur | Raam kaaj karibe ko aatur ||7||
You are supremely knowledgeable, virtuous, and intelligent. You are always eager to perform the tasks of Lord Rama.
प्रभु चरित्र सुनिबे को रसिया।
राम लखन सीता मन बसिया॥8॥
Prabhu charitra sunibe ko rasiya | Raam Lakhan Sita man basiya ||8||
You delight in listening to the stories of your Lord. You reside in the hearts of Rama, Lakshmana, and Sita.
सूक्ष्म रूप धरि सियहिं दिखावा।
बिकट रूप धरि लंक जरावा॥9॥
Sukshma roop dhari Siyahin dikhawa | Vikat roop dhari Lanka jarawa ||9||
Taking a subtle form, you appeared before Sita. Assuming a fierce form, you set Lanka ablaze.
भीम रूप धरि असुर संहारे।
रामचन्द्र के काज सँवारे॥10॥
Bheem roop dhari asur sanhaare | Ramchandra ke kaaj sanvare ||10||
Assuming a gigantic form, you destroyed the demons and accomplished all the tasks of Lord Ramachandra.
लाय सञ्जीवन लखन जियाये।
श्रीरघुबीर हरषि उर लाये॥11॥
Lay sanjeevan Lakhan jiyaaye | Shri Raghubeer harsh ur laaye ||11||
Bringing the Sanjivani herb, you revived Lakshmana. Lord Rama embraced you with great joy.
रघुपति कीन्ही बहुत बड़ाई।
तुम मम प्रिय भरतहि सम भाई॥12॥
Raghupati keenhi bahut badhaai | Tum mam priya Bharat hi sam bhaai ||12||
Lord Rama praised you greatly, saying, "You are as dear to me as my brother Bharata."
सहस बदन तुम्हरो जस गावैं।
अस कहि श्रीपति कंठ लगावैं॥13॥
Sahas badan tumharo jas gaavain | As kahi Shripati kanth lagaavain ||13||
Even the thousand-mouthed serpent sings your glory. Saying this, Lord Rama embraced you.
सनकादिक ब्रह्मादि मुनीसा।
नारद सारद सहित अहीसा॥14॥
Sanakaadik Brahmaadi muneesaa | Naarad Saarad sahita aheesaa ||14||
Sages like Sanaka, Brahma, and other great sages, along with Narada, Saraswati, and the serpent king, sing your praises.
जम कुबेर दिगपाल जहाँ ते।
कबि कोबिद कहि सके कहाँ ते॥15॥
Yam Kuber digpaal jahaan te | Kabi kovid kahi sake kahaan te ||15||
Even Yama, Kubera, and the guardians of the directions, along with poets and scholars, cannot fully describe your glory.
तुम उपकार सुग्रीवहिं कीन्हा।
राम मिलाय राज पद दीन्हा॥16॥
Tum upkaar Sugreevahin keenha | Raam milaay raj pad deenha ||16||
You did a great favor to Sugriva, uniting him with Lord Rama and granting him the kingship.
तुम्हरो मंत्र बिभीषन माना।
लंकेस्वर भए सब जग जाना॥17॥
Tumharo mantra Vibheeshan maana | Lankeshwar bhaye sab jag jaana ||17||
Vibhishana heeded your advice and became the king of Lanka, as known to the entire world.
जुग सहस्र जोजन पर भानू।
लील्यो ताहि मधुर फल जानू॥18॥
Yug sahastra jojan par bhaanu | Leelyo taahi madhur phal jaanu ||18||
The sun is thousands of miles away, yet you swallowed it, thinking it to be a sweet fruit.
प्रभु मुद्रिका मेलि मुख माहीं।
जलधि लाँघि गये अचरज नाहीं॥19॥
Prabhu mudrika meli mukh maahin | Jaldhi laanghi gaye acharaj naahin ||19||
Taking the Lord's ring in your mouth, you crossed the ocean. There is no wonder in this.
दुर्गम काज जगत के जेते।
सुगम अनुग्रह तुम्हरे तेते॥20॥
Durgam kaaj jagat ke jete | Sugam anugrah tumhare tete ||20||
All the difficult tasks in this world become easy by your grace.
राम दुआरे तुम रखवारे।
होत न आज्ञा बिनु पैसारे॥21॥
Raam duaare tum rakhwaare | Hot na aagya binu paisare ||21||
You are the guardian at the door of Lord Rama. No one can enter without your permission.
सब सुख लहै तुम्हारी सरना।
तुम रक्षक काहू को डरना॥22॥
Sab sukh lahai tumhaari sarna | Tum rakshak kaahu ko darnaa ||22||
All happiness is obtained by taking refuge in you. When you are the protector, what is there to fear?
आपन तेज सम्हारो आपै।
तीनों लोक हाँक तें काँपै॥23॥
Aapan tej samhaaro aapai | Teenon lok haanke te kaampai ||23||
You alone can control your own power. All three worlds tremble at your roar.
भूत पिशाच निकट नहिं आवै।
महाबीर जब नाम सुनावै॥24॥
Bhoot pishaach nikat nahin aavai | Mahaabeer jab naam sunaavai ||24||
Ghosts and evil spirits do not come near when one recites the name of the great warrior Hanuman.
नासै रोग हरै सब पीरा।
जपत निरंतर हनुमत बीरा॥25॥
Naasai rog harai sab peera | Japat nirantar Hanumat beera ||25||
All diseases and pains are destroyed by constantly chanting the name of the brave Hanuman.
संकट तें हनुमान छुड़ावै।
मन क्रम बचन ध्यान जो लावै॥26॥
Sankat te Hanuman chhudavai | Man kram vachan dhyaan jo laavai ||26||
Hanuman liberates from all difficulties those who meditate on him with mind, action, and speech.
सब पर राम तपस्वी राजा।
तिन के काज सकल तुम साजा॥27॥
Sab par Raam tapasvee raaja | Tin ke kaaj sakal tum saaja ||27||
Lord Rama is the king of all ascetics. You accomplished all his tasks.
और मनोरथ जो कोई लावै।
सोइ अमित जीवन फल पावै॥28॥
Aur manorath jo koi laavai | Soi amit jeevan phal paavai ||28||
Whoever comes with any desire obtains the abundant fruits of life.
चारों जुग परताप तुम्हारा।
है परसिद्ध जगत उजियारा॥29॥
Chaaron jug partaap tumhaara | Hai parsidh jagat ujiyaara ||29||
Your glory is known throughout the four ages. Your fame is renowned and illuminates the world.
साधु संत के तुम रखवारे।
असुर निकंदन राम दुलारे॥30॥
Saadhu sant ke tum rakhwaare | Asur nikandan Raam dulaare ||30||
You are the protector of saints and sages. You are the destroyer of demons and the beloved of Lord Rama.
अष्ट सिद्धि नौ निधि के दाता।
अस बर दीन्ह जानकी माता॥31॥
Ashta siddhi nau nidhi ke daata | As var deenha Jaanaki maata ||31||
You are the giver of the eight supernatural powers and nine treasures. Mother Janaki granted you this boon.
राम रसायन तुम्हरे पासा।
सदा रहो रघुपति के दासा॥32॥
Raam rasaayan tumhare paasa | Sada raho Raghupati ke daasa ||32||
You possess the essence of devotion to Rama. May you always remain the servant of the Lord of Raghus.
तुम्हरे भजन राम को पावै।
जनम जनम के दुख बिसरावै॥33॥
Tumhare bhajan Raam ko paavai | Janam janam ke dukh bisraavai ||33||
By singing your praises, one attains Lord Rama and forgets the sorrows of countless births.
अन्त काल रघुबर पुर जाई।
जहाँ जन्म हरि भक्त कहाई॥34॥
Ant kaal Raghubar pur jaaee | Jahaan janam Hari bhakt kahaaee ||34||
At the end of life, one goes to the abode of Lord Rama and is born as a devotee of the Lord wherever he takes birth.
और देवता चित्त न धरई।
हनुमत सेइ सर्ब सुख करई॥35॥
Aur devta chitta na dharai | Hanumat sei sarb sukh karai ||35||
One who serves Hanuman without paying attention to other deities attains all happiness.
संकट कटै मिटै सब पीरा।
जो सुमिरै हनुमत बलबीरा॥36॥
Sankat katai mitai sab peera | Jo sumirai Hanumat balbeera ||36||
All difficulties and pains are removed for one who remembers the mighty Hanuman.
जय जय जय हनुमान गोसाईं।
कृपा करहु गुरुदेव की नाईं॥37॥
Jai jai jai Hanuman gosaai | Kripa karahu gurudev kee naai ||37||
Victory, victory, victory to Lord Hanuman. Bestow your grace upon me as a spiritual master would.
जो सत बार पाठ कर कोई।
छूटहि बंदि महा सुख होई॥38॥
Jo sat baar paath kar koi | Chhootahi bandi maha sukh hoi ||38||
Whoever recites this one hundred times is liberated from bondage and attains great happiness.
जो यह पढ़ै हनुमान चालीसा।
होय सिद्धि साखी गौरीसा॥39॥
Jo yah padhe Hanuman Chaaleesaa | Hoy siddhi saakhi Gaurisaa ||39||
Whoever recites this Hanuman Chalisa attains perfection, with Lord Shiva as witness.
तुलसीदास सदा हरि चेरा।
कीजै नाथ हृदय महँ डेरा॥40॥
Tulsidas sada Hari chera | Keejai naath hridaya mah dera ||40||
Tulsidas, the eternal servant of the Lord, prays: O Lord, please dwell in my heart forever.
🙏 Closing Doha
पवन तनय संकट हरन, मंगल मूरति रूप।
राम लखन सीता सहित, हृदय बसहु सुर भूप॥
Pavan tanay sankat haran, mangal moorati roop | Raam Lakhan Sita sahit, hriday basahu sur bhoop ||
Meaning: O son of the wind god, remover of difficulties, embodiment of auspiciousness! May you reside in my heart along with Lord Rama, Lakshmana, and Sita, O king of gods.
📿 Phala-śruti — The Fruit of Recitation
पाठ करै हनुमान चालीसा।
सिद्ध होइ सब संकट बीसा॥
Paath karai Hanuman Chaaleesaa | Siddh hoi sab sankat beesaa ||
Meaning: One who recites the Hanuman Chalisa attains perfection and all twenty kinds of difficulties (physical, mental, and spiritual) are removed.

🙏 ॥ इति श्री हनुमान चालीसा सम्पूर्णम् ॥ 🙏
Thus ends the complete Hanuman Chalisa (all 40 verses).
May Lord Hanuman bless all who recite these sacred verses with devotion, strength, and protection.
