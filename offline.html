Offline Mode

First time setup needed

📿 To use this app offline, you need internet just once.

🌸 How to enable offline mode:
  • 1️⃣ Connect to the internet
  • 2️⃣ Refresh this page
  • 3️⃣ Tap Share button → "Add to Home Screen"
  • 4️⃣ Then it works offline forever!

🙏 Once installed, you can chant Sri Matre Namaha
even without internet — anytime, anywhere.
