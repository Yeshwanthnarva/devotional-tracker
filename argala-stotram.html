Argala Stotram

अर्गलास्तोत्रम् — The Hymn that Unlocks Divine Grace

Argala Stotram is a powerful hymn dedicated to Goddess Durga, often recited during the Chandi Path or Durga Saptashati. The stotram invokes the blessings of the Goddess in her various forms, asking for protection, prosperity, victory, and the removal of obstacles. The word "Argala" refers to a "bolt" or "lock," and just as a lock needs to be opened to gain access to something valuable, the recitation of the Argala Stotram unlocks the blessings and grace of the Divine Mother.

📜 Historical & Scriptural Context

The Argala Stotram is a part of the Durga Saptashati (Devi Mahatmyam) within the Markandeya Purana, which narrates the Goddess's battles with various demons — symbolizing the triumph of good over evil and the cosmic struggle between dharma and adharma. The Durga Saptashati contains 700 verses, and the Argala Stotram serves as a prelude or introduction to this sacred text. Reciting it before the Saptashati enhances spiritual benefits and the efficacy of the ritual.

📖 Structure & Meaning

The Argala Stotram consists of verses where each verse is a prayer asking the Goddess for specific boons. The refrain "rūpaṃ dehi, jayaṃ dehi, yaśo dehi, dviṣo jahi" repeats throughout, emphasizing the devotee's desire for inner beauty, victory, fame, and the destruction of enemies — both external and internal.

✨ Key Themes & Symbolism

🏆 Victory Over Evil 🛡️ Protection & Guidance 🌟 Fame, Fortune & Prosperity ⚔️ Removal of Obstacles & Enemies 🙏 Devotion & Surrender 🌸 Grace of the Goddess

Victory Over Evil: The Goddess is praised for victories over Mahishasura, Shumbha-Nishumbha, Raktabīja — representing ego, ignorance, greed, lust.
Protection & Guidance: The devotee turns to Durga for protection from physical, mental, and spiritual obstacles.
Fame, Fortune & Prosperity: Prayers for wealth, health, and success (both material and spiritual).
Removal of Obstacles & Enemies: Enemies symbolize doubt, fear, jealousy; by destroying them, the Goddess paves the way for success.
Devotion & Surrender: Acknowledges all blessings flow from Her grace.
Grace of the Goddess: She is Bhagavati, Jagadamba — the all-pervading force that bestows auspiciousness.

🕉️ Nyāsa — Invocation
ॐ अस्य श्री अर्गलास्तोत्रमन्त्रस्य विष्णुः ऋषिः।
अनुष्टुप् छन्दः। श्री महालक्ष्मीः देवता।
श्रीजगदम्बाप्रीतये सप्तशतीपाठाङ्गत्वेन जपे विनियोगः॥
ॐ नमश्चण्डिकायै॥
Om Asya Shri Argalastotramantrasya Vishnur Rishih | Anushtup Chandah | Shri Mahalakshmir Devata | Shri Jagadamba Preetaye Saptashati Paathangatvena Jape Viniyogah || Om Namash Chandikayai ||
Meaning: This is the dedication (Sankalpa). The seer (Rishi) is Vishnu, meter Anushtup, deity Śrī Mahālakṣmī. It is recited for the pleasure of Śrī Jagadambā (Mother of the Universe). We bow to Chandika (the fierce and victorious form of Devi).

📜 Mārkaṇḍeya Uvāca — Opening Hymn
ॐ जयन्ती मङ्गला काली भद्रकाली कपालिनी।
दुर्गा क्षमा शिवा धात्री स्वाहा स्वधा नमोऽस्तु ते॥
Om Jayanti Mangala Kali Bhadrakali Kapalini | Durga Kshama Shiva Dhatri Swaha Swadha Namostute ||
Salutations to You who are Jayanti (Ever-Victorious), Mangala (Auspicious), Kali (beyond Time), Bhadrakali, Kapalini, Durga (remover of difficulties), Kshama (Forgiveness), Shiva, Dhatri (Supporter), Svaha and Svadha. Salutations to You, O Divine Mother.
🌸
Verse 1
जय त्वं देवि चामुण्डे जय भूतार्तिहारिणि।
जय सर्वगते देवि कालरात्रि नमोऽस्तु ते॥
Jaya Tvam Devi Chamunde Jaya Bhutarti-harini | Jaya Sarvagate Devi Kalaratri Namostute ||
Victory to You, O Devi Chamunda! Victory to You who removes the sufferings of all beings! Victory to You, all-pervading Goddess, O Kalaratri — salutations to You.
Verse 2
मधुकैटभविद्राविविधात्रि वरदे नमः।
रूपं देहि जयं देहि यशो देहि द्विषो जहि॥
Madhu-Kaitabha Vidravi Vidhatri Vara-de Namah | Roopam Dehi Jayam Dehi Yasho Dehi Dvisho Jahi ||
Salutations to You who destroyed Madhu and Kaitabha and granted boons to Brahma. Grant me beauty, grant me victory, grant me fame, and destroy my enemies.
Verse 3
महिषासुरनिर्नाशि भक्तानां सुखदे नमः।
रूपं देहि जयं देहि यशो देहि द्विषो जहि॥
Mahishasura Nirnashi Bhaktanam Sukhade Namah | Roopam Dehi Jayam Dehi Yasho Dehi Dvisho Jahi ||
Salutations to You who annihilated Mahishasura (ego & inertia) and bestow joy upon devotees. O Devi — grant me Beauty, Victory, Glory, destroy my enemies.
Verse 4
रक्तबीजवधे देवि चण्डमुण्डविनाशिनि।
रूपं देहि जयं देहि यशो देहि द्विषो जहि॥
Raktabija-vadhe Devi Chanda-Munda Vinashini | Roopam Dehi Jayam Dehi Yasho Dehi Dvisho Jahi ||
O slayer of Raktabīja (recurring negative tendencies) and destroyer of Chanda-Munda — grant me Beauty, Victory, Glory, destroy my enemies.
Verse 5
शुम्भस्यैव निशुम्भस्य धूम्राक्षस्य च मर्दिनि।
रूपं देहि जयं देहि यशो देहि द्विषो जहि॥
Shumbhasyaiva Nishumbhasya Dhumrakshasya Cha Mardini | Roopam Dehi Jayam Dehi Yasho Dehi Dvisho Jahi ||
O destroyer of Śumbha, Niśumbha (pride & ego) and Dhūmrākṣa (delusion) — grant me Beauty, Victory, Glory, destroy my enemies.
Verse 6
वन्दिताङ्घ्रियुगे देवि सर्वसौभाग्यदायिनि।
रूपं देहि जयं देहि यशो देहि द्विषो जहि॥
Vanditanghri-yuge Devi Sarva-saubhagya-dayini | Roopam Dehi Jayam Dehi Yasho Dehi Dvisho Jahi ||
O Goddess whose feet are worshipped, bestower of all good fortune — grant me Beauty, Victory, Glory, destroy my enemies.
Verse 7
अचिन्त्यरूपचरिते सर्वशत्रुविनाशिनि।
रूपं देहि जयं देहि यशो देहि द्विषो जहि॥
Achintya-roopa-charite Sarva-shatru-vinashini | Roopam Dehi Jayam Dehi Yasho Dehi Dvisho Jahi ||
O Goddess of inconceivable form & deeds, destroyer of all enemies — grant me Beauty, Victory, Glory, destroy my enemies.
Verse 8
नतेभ्यः सर्वदा भक्त्या चण्डिके दुरितापहे।
रूपं देहि जयं देहि यशो देहि द्विषो जहि॥
Natebhyah Sarvada Bhaktya Chandike Duritapaha | Roopam Dehi Jayam Dehi Yasho Dehi Dvisho Jahi ||
O Chandika, remover of sorrows for those who bow with devotion — grant me Beauty, Victory, Glory, destroy my enemies.
Verse 9
स्तुवद्भ्यो भक्तिपूर्वं त्वां चण्डिके व्याधिनाशिनि।
रूपं देहि जयं देहि यशो देहि द्विषो जहि॥
Stuvadbhyo Bhakti-poorvam Tvam Chandike Vyadhi-nashini | Roopam Dehi Jayam Dehi Yasho Dehi Dvisho Jahi ||
O Chandika, destroyer of diseases, praised with devotion — grant me Beauty, Victory, Glory, destroy my enemies.
Verse 10
चण्डिके सततं ये त्वामर्चयन्तीह भक्तितः।
रूपं देहि जयं देहि यशो देहि द्विषो जहि॥
Chandike Satatam Ye Tvam Archayantiha Bhaktitah | Roopam Dehi Jayam Dehi Yasho Dehi Dvisho Jahi ||
O Chandika, those who constantly worship You with devotion — grant me Beauty, Victory, Glory, destroy my enemies.
Verse 11
देहि सौभाग्यमारोग्यं देहि मे परमं सुखम्।
रूपं देहि जयं देहि यशो देहि द्विषो जहि॥
Dehi Saubhagyam Arogyam Dehi Me Paramam Sukham | Roopam Dehi Jayam Dehi Yasho Dehi Dvisho Jahi ||
Grant me good fortune, health, and supreme happiness. Grant me Beauty, Victory, Glory, destroy my enemies.
Verse 12
विधेहि द्विषतां नाशं विधेहि बलमुच्चकैः।
रूपं देहि जयं देहि यशो देहि द्विषो जहि॥
Vidhehi Dvishatam Nasham Vidhehi Balam Uchchaih | Roopam Dehi Jayam Dehi Yasho Dehi Dvisho Jahi ||
Bring destruction to my enemies, grant me great strength. Grant me Beauty, Victory, Glory, destroy my enemies.
Verse 13
विधेहि देवि कल्याणं विधेहि परमां श्रियम्।
रूपं देहि जयं देहि यशो देहि द्विषो जहि॥
Vidhehi Devi Kalyanam Vidhehi Paramam Shriyam | Roopam Dehi Jayam Dehi Yasho Dehi Dvisho Jahi ||
O Goddess, bestow auspiciousness and supreme prosperity. Grant me Beauty, Victory, Glory, destroy my enemies.
Verse 14
सुरासुरशिरोरत्ननिघृष्टचरणेऽम्बिके।
रूपं देहि जयं देहि यशो देहि द्विषो जहि॥
Surasura-shiro-ratna Nighrushta-charanembike | Roopam Dehi Jayam Dehi Yasho Dehi Dvisho Jahi ||
O Mother Ambika, whose feet are touched by jewelled crowns of gods & demons — grant me Beauty, Victory, Glory, destroy my enemies.
Verse 15
विद्यावन्तं यशस्वन्तं लक्ष्मीवन्तं जनं कुरु।
रूपं देहि जयं देहि यशो देहि द्विषो जहि॥
Vidyavantam Yashasvantam Lakshmivantam Janam Kuru | Roopam Dehi Jayam Dehi Yasho Dehi Dvisho Jahi ||
Make me learned, famous, and prosperous. Grant me Beauty, Victory, Glory, destroy my enemies.
Verse 16
प्रचण्डदैत्यदर्पघ्ने चण्डिके प्रणताय मे।
रूपं देहि जयं देहि यशो देहि द्विषो जहि॥
Prachanda-daitya-darpa-ghne Chandike Pranataaya Me | Roopam Dehi Jayam Dehi Yasho Dehi Dvisho Jahi ||
O Chandika, destroyer of fierce demon pride, I bow to You — grant me Beauty, Victory, Glory, destroy my enemies.
Verse 17
चतुर्भुजे चतुर्वक्त्रसंस्तुते परमेश्वरि।
रूपं देहि जयं देहि यशो देहि द्विषो जहि॥
Chaturbhuje Chaturvaktra-Sanstute Parameshwari | Roopam Dehi Jayam Dehi Yasho Dehi Dvisho Jahi ||
O four-armed Supreme Goddess, praised by four-faced Brahma — grant me Beauty, Victory, Glory, destroy my enemies.
Verse 18
कृष्णेन संस्तुते देवि शश्वद्भक्त्या सदाम्बिके।
रूपं देहि जयं देहि यशो देहि द्विषो जहि॥
Krishnena Samstute Devi Shashvad-bhaktya Sadaambike | Roopam Dehi Jayam Dehi Yasho Dehi Dvisho Jahi ||
O Devi Ambika, eternally praised by Lord Krishna — grant me Beauty, Victory, Glory, destroy my enemies.
Verse 19
हिमाचलसुतानाथसंस्तुते परमेश्वरि।
रूपं देहि जयं देहि यशो देहि द्विषो जहि॥
Himachala-sutanatha-Sanstute Parameshwari | Roopam Dehi Jayam Dehi Yasho Dehi Dvisho Jahi ||
O Supreme Goddess praised by Shiva (Lord of Himavan's daughter) — grant me Beauty, Victory, Glory, destroy my enemies.
Verse 20
इन्द्राणीपतिसद्भावपूजिते परमेश्वरि।
रूपं देहि जयं देहि यशो देहि द्विषो जहि॥
Indrani-pati-sadbhaava-Poojite Parameshwari | Roopam Dehi Jayam Dehi Yasho Dehi Dvisho Jahi ||
O Parameshvari, worshipped by Indra with true devotion — grant me Beauty, Victory, Glory, destroy my enemies.
Verse 21
देवि प्रचण्डदोर्दण्डदैत्यदर्पविनाशिनि।
रूपं देहि जयं देहि यशो देहि द्विषो जहि॥
Devi Prachanda-dordanda-Daitya-darpa-vinashini | Roopam Dehi Jayam Dehi Yasho Dehi Dvisho Jahi ||
O Goddess who destroys demon pride with mighty arms — grant me Beauty, Victory, Glory, destroy my enemies.
Verse 22
देवि भक्तजनोद्दामदत्तानन्दोदयेम्बिके।
रूपं देहि जयं देहि यशो देहि द्विषो जहि॥
Devi Bhakta-janoddaama-Dattaanandodayembike | Roopam Dehi Jayam Dehi Yasho Dehi Dvisho Jahi ||
O Mother Ambika, giver of great joy to devotees — grant me Beauty, Victory, Glory, destroy my enemies.
Verse 23 — Prayer for Noble Life
पत्नीं मनोरमां देहि मनोवृत्तानुसारिणीम्।
तारिणीं दुर्गसंसारसागरस्य कुलोद्भवाम्॥
Patnim Manoramam Dehi Mano-vritta-anusarini | Tarini Durga Samsara-Sagarasya Kulodbhavam ||
Grant me a delightful companion aligned with dharma, who helps me cross the ocean of samsara, born of noble lineage. (Spiritually: a mind attuned to virtue, and divine grace that liberates.)

🌟 Phala-śruti — The Fruit of Recitation
इदं स्तोत्रं पठित्वा तु महास्तोत्रं पठेन्नरः।
स तु सप्तशतीसंख्यावरमाप्नोति सम्पदम्॥
Idam Stotram Pathitva Tu Maha-stotram Pathen Narah | Sa Tu Saptashati-sankhya Varam Apnoti Sampadam ||
Having recited this Argala Stotram, one should read the Maha Stotram (Durga Saptashati). He attains the merit equal to reciting the entire Saptashati and achieves great prosperity — both material and spiritual.

🙏 iti Devyāḥ Argalāstotraṃ Sampūrṇam 🙏
Thus ends the complete Argala Stotram of the Goddess.
May the Divine Mother bless all who recite these sacred verses with devotion.
