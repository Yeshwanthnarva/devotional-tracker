About This App

Built with devotion — for the path of Moksha
"The purpose of human life is to attain Self-Realisation — to know who we truly are beyond the body, mind and ego. Daily japa is one of the most powerful tools on this path."

🌸 Purpose of This App

This Devotional Tracker was created with one simple and sacred purpose — to help a sincere devotee track their daily Japa (mantra chanting) and stay consistent on the path of spiritual practice.

The chosen mantra is Sri Matre Namaha — a salutation to the Divine Mother, meaning "Salutations to the Holy Mother." It is a mantra of surrender, love, and devotion to the Supreme Feminine principle — Shakti herself.

The minimum daily goal is 108 repetitions (1 mala). The number 108 is sacred in Hinduism — it represents the distance between Earth and Sun (in solar diameters), the number of Upanishads, the number of names of many deities, and the mathematical relationship of the universe itself.

🕉️ What is Moksha?

Moksha (also called Mukti or Liberation) is the ultimate goal of human existence according to Hindu philosophy. It means freedom from the endless cycle of birth, death, and rebirth (Samsara).

When a soul attains Moksha, it merges back into the Supreme Consciousness — like a drop of water merging into the ocean. There is no more suffering, no more separation from the Divine.

The Bhagavad Gita, the Upanishads, and all the great saints of India teach that Moksha is not something far away — it is our very nature. We only need to remove the layers of ignorance (Maya) that cover it.

"You are not a human being having a spiritual experience. You are a spiritual being having a human experience." — This is the core teaching of Vedanta.

Daily japa, meditation, selfless service (Seva), and devotion (Bhakti) are the paths that gradually purify the mind and lead to this ultimate realisation.

🪬 The Power of Japa

Japa means the repetitive chanting of a divine name or mantra. It is one of the most ancient and powerful spiritual practices in the world.

When we chant Sri Matre Namaha with sincerity, we are:

— Surrendering our ego to the Divine Mother
— Cleansing the mind of negative impressions (Samskaras)
— Building a direct inner connection with the Supreme
— Gradually dissolving the sense of separation between self and God

The great saint Ramana Maharshi said: "The name of God chanted correctly or incorrectly, knowingly or unknowingly, carefully or carelessly — is sure to give the desired result."

108 repetitions daily, done consistently over years, is a profound sadhana (spiritual practice) that many saints and sages recommend as a foundation for the spiritual life.

📱 How to Use This App

Tap Mode — Simply tap the "+1 Tap" button each time you silently or audibly chant the mantra. This is the most natural and recommended mode, especially for phone use.

Typing Mode — Type the mantra "Sri Matre Namaha" and press Submit. You can type it multiple times in the text box and submit once — the app will count each occurrence.

Timer — The timer starts automatically when you begin chanting and pauses after 2 seconds of inactivity. Your total time is saved to the cloud.

Sync — All data is stored securely in Google Firebase. You can use the same account on your phone and laptop — the data syncs in real time.

Streak — Your daily streak is tracked. Try to do at least 108 chants every day to maintain your streak!
